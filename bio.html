BIOGRAPHY

MEGAN HAS STUDIED CLASSICAL VOICE FOR OVER ELEVEN YEARS. SHE HAS A BACHELOR OF ARTS DEGREE IN MUSIC FROM THE UNIVERSITY OF UTAH WITH AN EMPHASIS IN VOCAL PERFORMANCE. MEGAN HAS SUNG IN A CATHEDRAL CHORAL SETTING SINCE THE AGE OF TEN AND IS AN ALUMNA OF THE MADELEINE CHOIR SCHOOL. SHE HAS LONG BEEN INVOLVED WITH MANY PERFORMANCE ENSEMBLES, OPERAS, MUSICALS, AND LITURGICAL SERVICES THROUGHOUT THE SALT LAKE VALLEY. SHE CURRENTLY HOLDS THE TITLE OF ASSISTANT CANTOR AT THE CATHEDRAL OF THE MADELEINE. SHE LOVES TO CONTINUALLY IMPROVE HER SINGING AND TEACHING ABILITIES, AND RECENTLY ATTENDED THE ALEXANDER TECHNIQUE INTERNATIONAL WORKSHOP IN NORTH CAROLINA FOCUSING ON ALEXANDER TECHNIQUE FOR THE SINGER.
